About soccer

Soccer is a well known game around the world. It's a sport that play with 11 player in the field, with At least 5 Substitutions in the bench, but a match could start or continue to play with 7 player.

Famous player and legend

Some of the most well-known include Pelé, Diego Maradona, Lionel Messi, and Cristiano Ronaldo. Other prominent figures from the past include Johan Cruyff, Franz Beckenbauer, and Zinedine Zidane.
